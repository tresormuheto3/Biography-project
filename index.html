Joseph Olaitan Adenuga Jr

known professionally as Skepta, born on September 19, 1982 in London's Tottenham district, is a pioneering grime rapper and British music producer from Nigeria. He founded the Meridian Crew collective, named after the street where he lived in Tottenham.

Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun

aka Wizkid, is a Nigerian singer-songwriter, born on July 16, 1990 in Surulere, Lagos State. He began recording music at the age of 11. He rose to fame in 2010 with the release of the song Holla at Your Boy, followed by his first studio album, Superstar in 2011.

Pharrell Lanscilo Williams

known as Pharrell, is an American singer-songwriter, musician, producer, stylist, rapper and producer born on April 5, 1973 in Virginia Beach, Virginia, USA. With Chad Hugo, they form the music production duo The Neptunes.His first single Frontin' was released in 2003.
